Breeds of Cats

Cats are lovable domesticated mammals which are now one of the most common household animals.

The munchkin can is characterized by it's genetic mutation which causes the cat to have a stocky body and short leg

The ragdoll cat is a cat typically breeded in America which has a dense coat and blue eyes.

The Scotish fold cat is known as the domesticated British household cat.

Cats are part of many people's day-to-day lives, they act as companions, friends, and family to many.
